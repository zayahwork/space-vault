4. **The daily run is automated.** Scheduled task, every 6h, appends the verdict — including
   "quiet, nothing happened" days. *"The July 29 demo week is now building itself."*
5. **Jul 29 is coming.** `quiet.py` — catching a satellite that *stopped* maneuvering —
   unlocks when the archive hits 7 days. Ends the episode on a countdown.

## Standing capture habits (from here on)

- **Film the start of a work block before you know how it ends.** That's the only way to
  get a genuine reaction, and it means you never have to fake one later.
- **One 20-second "where I'm at" to camera at the end of every work session.** Cheap, and
  it's the narration spine of every future episode.
- **Keep OBS rolling** — you already do. Screen capture is free B-roll.
