## Jargon, once
- **bar / baseline** = what a normal orbit-vs-catalog gap looks like, learned from past
  days and frozen; only something *beyond* that gets flagged.
- **verified** = a second, unrelated method (30 days of Space-Track altitude history) also
  saw movement near the same time.
