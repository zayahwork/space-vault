# 🔍 Guide — The 4 Competitor Papers (AMOS 2025)

Four teams published on OUR exact niche at the field's main conference last year. Abstracts are
free at `amostech.space/amos/2025/` (full PDFs are paywalled — abstracts are enough for now).

**Why bother:** (1) proof the niche is hot; (2) learn the vocabulary buyers/experts use;
(3) every author is future outreach ammo.

| Paper | Who | Takeaway to hunt for |
|---|---|---|
| *Evaluating Maneuver Pattern of Life Violations using Unsupervised Learning and Object History* | **Digantara** (written by their founders) | How they define a "violation" of normal behavior |
| *A Three Pillar Algorithm Pipeline for Low Latency Space Object Maneuver Detection* | **Advanced Space** (Patrick Miga — our fallback contact) | What "low latency" means to them & why it's hard |
| *Expanding Pattern-of-Life Capabilities on Satellite Passive RF Datasets* | **Kratos** | PoL from radio signals (different sensor, same idea) |
| *Evaluating Photometric Fingerprinting and Change Detection at Scale* | **Slingshot** (Brian Williams — we emailed him) | Change detection at scale — closest to Agatha |

**While reading, keep one question in mind:** *what do all four need that free catalog data
can't give them — and what can free data do that they're ignoring?* Write answers in the log.
