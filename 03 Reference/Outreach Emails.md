## Small things that keep these from sounding fake
- Send from your real address with your real name and a phone number. An empty signature reads bot.
- Change at least one sentence per email so they're not identical. The specific detail line matters most.
- Short subject, lowercase is fine, no "Exploring Synergies."
- If you don't hear back, one short nudge after ~3 business days ("bumping this up in case it got buried"), then let it go.
