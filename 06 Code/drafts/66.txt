Briefly, what I do: ML on orbital data, built from free public tracking data only. A manoeuvre detector that ranks objects which broke their own routine, checked against the catalog's own altitude history with a matched control group instead of grading itself - about two-thirds of the top candidates show independent movement against a ~10% control base rate.
